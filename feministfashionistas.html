FEMINIST FASHIONISTAS.

Fashion is finally fit for modern women.Utility would not be a trend if not for these women. We have listed our favourite strong fashion icons to keep your eye on.

YOON AHN

Born in Seattle, Yoon Ahn is the Korean American founder of the hippest streetwear, Ambush. Her lush collaboration with Nike is to die for! Ahn's couture jewellery line called Antonio Murphy & Astro is getting a lot of high demand and recognition globally.

VICTORIA BECKHAM

She may have entered the cultural lexicon back in the 1990s—first as a member of the bubblegum-pop phenomenon the Spice Girls, then as the wife of the lady-killing soccer player David Beckham—but Victoria Beckham only truly earned her nickname when she took to the London catwalk for designer Maria Grachvogel in February 2000, sporting hot pants and a flamenco dress.

ISABEL TOLEDO

Isabel Toledo’s Midtown studio functions as a visual history of her remarkable life—a sort of creative’s fairy-tale romance that’s inextricably intertwined with her husband, Ruben Toledo, whom she began dating as a teenager. Ruben’s sketches for her clothing line cover the walls. Tiles encircling the staircase and adorning the powder room, painted by Ruben, are called The Isabel Collection.
