Womyn

For your daily WCW needs! Here's to de-beiging our insta feeds one inspirational woman at a time. Whether you're into food, fashion or finance the women here will be your guiding light!

INDYA MOORE

Delivering inspiration and education, Indya uses her platform to shift the culture and conversations we have about the trans community. If you haven't yet jumped on the bandwagon that is Pose...its definately worth a watch!

MARIA QAMAR

Creating art that depicts the bittersweet truths of being part of the Desi diaspora, Maria uses her platform (hatecopy) to discuss Asian culture; from its taboos to its appropriation within western popculture...YES! We're looking at your Gwen Stefani circa 2003

VIOLETTE

Championing the wave of 'natural beauty',Violette has created a beautifully inclusive platform for all things beauty and fashion related.

KIMBERLY CRENSHAW

Legal scholar Kimberlé Crenshaw combines literature on critical race theory such as in her essay, “Demarginalizing the Intersection of Race and Sex, to examine antiracist and feminist discourse on women of color as victims sexual violence, arguing that racism and sexism act as mutually interlocking systems of oppression

LAVERNE COX

American actress and LGBTQ+ advocate. She is most known as Sophia Burset on the Netflix series Orange Is the New Black and, becoming the first openly transgender person to be nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award in an acting category.

TONI MORRISON

She is an american writer, noted for her examination of black experience (particularly black female experience) within the black community. She received the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1993. Morrison’s notable books included Beloved, Song of Solomon and The Bluest Eye.
